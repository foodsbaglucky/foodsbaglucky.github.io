our story

The trail food aisle never looked like my lunchbox.

Growing up in my family's Chinese restaurant, the best meals came out of white takeout boxes before the doors even opened. Staff meal. Real food. The kind that warms you from the inside out.

As a trail runner, snowboarder, and camper, I kept looking for that feeling outside. I never found it. So I made it. Lucky Bag is Chinese comfort food for the trail — starting with congee.
