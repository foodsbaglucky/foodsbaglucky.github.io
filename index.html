our story

Every trail meal I've ever packed tasted like someone else's kitchen

Before the restaurant doors opened every morning, my family ate congee. Simple rice, ginger, whatever was on hand. It was the meal that started every day. Quiet, warm, restorative.

Years later, I kept thinking about that bowl. Nothing in the trail food aisle came close. So I made it portable. Lucky Bag is that morning bowl, made for wherever your day takes you.
